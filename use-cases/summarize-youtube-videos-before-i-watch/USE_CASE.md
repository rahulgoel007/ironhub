### 1. Title

Summarize YouTube videos before I watch

### 2. Example prompt

"Summarize this YouTube video and tell me if it's worth watching."

### 3. What the agent does

Fetches the video transcript and extracts the key ideas, arguments, and conclusions. It produces a structured summary with the main takeaways, a list of timestamped highlights so you can jump straight to the parts that matter, and a short verdict on whether the video is worth your time given your stated goal. Useful summaries are optionally saved to your notes for future reference.

### 4. Skills & tools used

- YouTube Transcript tool — fetches the full video transcript without requiring playback
- Video Summary skill — distills the transcript into key points, timestamps, and a concise verdict
- Notes tool — saves useful summaries and highlights for later retrieval
